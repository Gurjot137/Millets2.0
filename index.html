About Khader Millets (N-WAY)

Khader Millets — also known as N-WAY (Nanak Way / Nature Way) — is a family-run millet business committed to sustainable farming and honest pricing. We pack and deliver quality millets with care. Contact us for bulk orders and retail supply.

Our Traditional Processing

We process millets using the traditional Buchchi-method to retain the top layer (Lignans), which have powerful anti-cancer, antibacterial, antifungal, and antiviral properties. This ensures maximum nutrition and authentic taste, just as recommended by Dr. Khadar Valli.
